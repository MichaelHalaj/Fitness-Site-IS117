About

With three years of experience in weightlifting, I embarked on my journey by diving into structured training programs like push/pull/legs, upper/lower splits, and full-body routines. These methodologies served as the foundation for my exploration into strength training, allowing me to progressively challenge my muscles while honing proper form and technique. From mastering compound lifts to refining isolation exercises, each session brought me closer to my fitness goals. Through dedication and consistency, I've not only witnessed physical transformations but also cultivated a deep passion for the art of lifting weights.
